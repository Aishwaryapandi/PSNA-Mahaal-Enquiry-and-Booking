About Us

Welcome to PSNA MAHAAL, your go-to platform for seamless venue management and booking services. We understand that finding the perfect venue for your events is crucial, and our mission is to simplify this process by providing a user-friendly solution that connects you with the ideal location to make your special occasions memorable.

Our platform is designed to cater to all types of events, be it weddings, corporate meetings, birthday parties, or any other gathering that requires a spacious and elegant setting. We partner with a wide range of venues to ensure that our users have access to the best choices in town. From traditional wedding halls to modern banquet facilities, we provide detailed information, availability, and easy booking options.

Amenities

Temple

Subhamangala Vinayagar Temple within the premises (as per Vasthu and Aagama Shashram).

Function Hall

A multipurpose function hall that is perfect for Weddings, Birthday Parties, Receptions, Seemantham, Upanayanam, Mehendi Parties, and other special occasions. Spacious elevated stage area for conduct to ceremonies with seating capacity in the function hall of 1000 people.

Dining Hall

Great ventilation. Spacious dining hall with comfortable tables and chairs, has seating capacity for 300+ people.

Catering

We are affiliated with multiple caterers in the city who are specialists in a wide range of cuisines and thus have the expertise to customise the perfect menu for all your events. All services, right from setting-up tables, using the apt cutlery, to serving food at the event - is taken care of.

Kitchen

Latest vessels to cook for 4000+ people. Well-equipped and well-ventilated spacious kitchen. Additional kitchen and storage facility.

Rooms

Furnished A/C rooms with modern facilities for bride and groom.

Light and Sound

One of the key elements at a function that lends to its success is the light and sound. At AM Mahal, we have partners who can cater to these requirements too. Our service providers have been delivering unique light and sound solutions for decades with well-qualified, trained professionals.

Parking

Large parking space for both bikes and cars.

Safety Vault

24 hr CCTV Surveillance, Emergency Lights, and Fire Safety Equipment.

Guest Rooms

5 Guest rooms available with A/C and hot water facilities.
